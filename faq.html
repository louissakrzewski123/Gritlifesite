We understand that payment for activities can often be confusing in typical care scenarios. To ensure full transparency and to simplify the process, we've categorised our activities into paid and free options, which are accessible with a Companion Card. This clear categorisation allows clients to easily understand which activities are covered and which might require payment, ensuring that no one is unexpectedly left out of pocket. Our goal is to make sure that all clients can participate in activities without financial surprises, allowing everyone to choose adventures that fit within their NDIS plan or personal budget
