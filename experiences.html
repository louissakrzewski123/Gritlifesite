TRAMPOLINING

Trampolining is a dynamic and exhilarating sport that combines fun, fitness, and a sense of weightless freedom. Originally developed as a training tool for astronauts and athletes, trampolining has grown into a popular recreational activity and competitive sport enjoyed by people of all ages. With its unique combination of acrobatics, strength, and balance, trampolining offers an engaging way to stay active and entertained.

LONE PINE KOALA SANCTUARY

Lone Pine Koala Sanctuary, located in Brisbane, Australia, is the world’s oldest and largest koala sanctuary, providing a unique and intimate opportunity to connect with some of Australia’s most beloved wildlife. Established in 1927, Lone Pine is not only a sanctuary for koalas but also home to a wide variety of native species, including kangaroos, wombats, dingoes, and birds, making it a must-visit destination for animal lovers and nature enthusiasts alike.

SOUTH BANK BEACH

South Bank Beach, located in the heart of Brisbane, Australia, is a unique man-made beach that offers a perfect blend of relaxation, entertainment, and natural beauty in an urban setting. Known as Streets Beach, this inner-city lagoon provides locals and tourists alike with a chance to enjoy a refreshing swim, sunbathe on sandy shores, or simply soak in the vibrant atmosphere, all without leaving the city. Whether you're looking for a family-friendly day out, a romantic escape, or a break from sightseeing, South Bank Beach offers something for everyone.

GOLD COAST BEACH TRIP

A trip to the Gold Coast is a quintessential Australian experience, offering sun-kissed beaches, thrilling waves, and a vibrant beach culture that draws visitors from all over the world. Located just an hour south of Brisbane, the Gold Coast boasts some of the best beaches in the country, making it an ideal destination for those seeking relaxation, adventure, or both. With over 57 kilometers of pristine coastline, a Gold Coast beach trip promises stunning views, endless outdoor activities, and unforgettable memories.
